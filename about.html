Welcome to E.C.I Group

17 Years of Experience

Engineering Chemical Industries S.A." is a Tunisian company that is a leader in the field of industrial process engineering with good international experience.

The company, Engineering Chemical Industries, is the builder of three companies: Chlorine Derivative Industries, NITROKYM, and La Tuniso-Algérienne de Chlore, and is a shareholder in all three. E.C.I. is a Public Limited Company and has been established in Tunisia since 2003 in the chemical industry sector, with a capital of 1,380,000 DT.

The main activity of E.C.I is engineering, supply and production of:

  • Sodium Hypochlorite (48 to 50 °Chl)
  • Calcium Hypochlorite
  • Caustic Soda 32%
  • Ferrous Chloride 40%
  • Dicalcium Phosphate (DCP)

The E.C.I Group employs 200 employees, including 10 senior managers, 30 engineers, 100 senior technicians, and 60 skilled workers with excellent technical knowledge and strong analytical ability.
